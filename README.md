# Telco Customer Churn Analysis Using Python

### Objective

- To analyze customer data and identify the main factors responsible for customer churn
- To understand customer behavior and suggest strategies to improve retention

### Key Insights 

- Overall customer churn rate is approximately 26‚Äì27%, which shows a serious retention challenge.
- Customers with Month-to-Month contracts have the highest churn rate compared to 1-year and 2-year contract customers.
- Senior Citizens show higher churn percentage than non-senior customers.
- Customers using Fiber Optic internet service churn more compared to those using DSL.
- Customers who do not have services like Online Security, Tech Support, and Device Protection have much higher churn rates.
- Customers with higher Monthly Charges are more likely to churn.
- Customers using electronic check as a payment method churn more than other payment methods.
- Longer-tenure customers show lower churn and stronger loyalty.

### Tools & Technologies Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

### Business Recommendations
- Provide special discount plans to Month-to-Month contract customers to reduce churn.
- Encourage customers to take 1-year or 2-year contracts by giving offers.
- Provide bundled security services (Online Security, Tech Support, Device Protection) at discounted prices.
- Improve service quality and pricing for Fiber Optic users.
- Create loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
- Offer special plans for Senior Citizens to increase retention.
- Introduce offers for customers with high Monthly Charges to reduce churn.
